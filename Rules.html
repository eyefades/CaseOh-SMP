Minecraft Server Rules

Welcome to the Minecraft Server Rules Wiki! This page outlines all the rules and guidelines for maintaining a respectful and enjoyable gaming environment on our server. Please read through the rules carefully and ensure you follow them to keep our community safe and fun for everyone.

1. Treat Others with Respect

  • No Harassment: Do not harass anyone on the server.
  • Report Harassment: Instead of retaliating, report harassment. Use /ignore if needed.
  • Respect Blocking/Ignoring: Respect others' wishes if they block or ignore you.

2. No Griefing Anywhere

  • Griefing Prohibited: Includes both claimed and unclaimed land, and revenge griefing.
  • Resource World Exception: Claims are disabled. Unattended items are considered abandoned and free to take. Use at your own risk.
  • Mass Destruction: Mass destruction of the map is considered griefing, except in the Resource World (/wild).
  • No Lava Casting: Lava casting is prohibited.

3. Keep Communications Clean

  • Prohibited Behaviors: Harassment, racism, excessive swearing, sexual content, and hate speech are not allowed.
  • Sexual Content: Inappropriate usernames, builds, signage, content, skins, and renamed items are prohibited.
  • Sensitive Topics: Topics like self-harm, politics, drug use, and abuse are not allowed in public chat.
  • Private Chat: Allowed for any topic if all parties consent.
  • Moderation Compliance: Do not ignore staff members to facilitate moderation.

4. No Hacking/X-ray/Exploiting

  • Prohibited Hacks: X-ray, botting, kill aura, fly, and free-cam/free-look are not allowed.
  • Allowed Mods: Fullbright, Lunar Client (no free-look), Mini-map, Optifine, Performance/FPS increasers are allowed.
  • Schematics Mods: Litematica is allowed, but auto-place functionality is prohibited.
  • Exploits: Report and do not use any exploits. Rewards may be given for reporting.

5. No Automation

  • Prohibited Automation: Botting (Baritone), auto-clickers, key weights are not allowed.
  • Allowed Automation: Farms requiring AFK or manual work are allowed.
  • Fishing Machines: Prohibited, even if the mouse is manually held down.

6. No Spamming/Begging/Advertising

  • Economy Advertising: Allowed every 15 minutes.
  • Prohibited Advertising: Links or outside advertisements, including other servers and external streams, are not allowed.
  • Spamming: Excessive capitalization, repetitive messages, and random letters are prohibited.

7. No Random PvP

  • PvP Consent: PvP must be consented to. Set and follow agreed rules.
  • PvP Zones: Entering PvP zones implies consent.
  • TP/Baiting: Tping or baiting players into PvP areas is prohibited.
  • Reporting PvP Issues: Report players instead of retaliating in unclear fights.

8. English Only in Chat

  • Public Chat: Only English is allowed to facilitate moderation.
  • Private Messages: Non-English is allowed in private messages (/msg <username>).

9. Claims

  • Inactivity: Claims can be removed for inactivity (3+ weeks) via a Discord ticket.
  • Building Near Claims: Do not build within 100 blocks of another claim without permission.
  • Claim Violations: Report claim violations promptly.
  • Trespassing: Respect the claim owner’s wishes; repeated trespassing will be punished.

10. No Scamming/Fraud/Impersonation

  • Scamming: Misrepresentation of goods or failing to follow through with trades is prohibited.
  • Allowed Trading: Low-balling and high-balling are allowed. Know item values before trading.
  • Impersonation: Impersonation using /nick is prohibited.
  • IRL Trading: No IRL trading for in-game items.

11. Shop and Economy

  • Advertising Near Shops: No advertising near other shops without permission.
  • Prohibited Listings: Useless/troll player warps, auction house listings, or chest shops are not allowed.
  • Main End Island: Reserved for travel and fighting the dragon only.

12. Alt-Accounts

  • Alt-Accounts: Not allowed.

13. Do Not Evade Punishments

  • Ban Evasion: Evading bans with alternate accounts will escalate the ban length.
  • Mute Evasion: Avoid using signs or books to bypass chat mutes.

14. VPN Usage

  • VPNs: VPNs are prohibited. If used, it’s at the player’s risk. VPN use may associate you with banned players.

15. Lag Rules

  • Efficient Builds: Build efficient and useful machines to minimize lag.
  • Turn Off Machines: Turn off unused machines.
  • Chunk Loaders: No chunk loaders are allowed.

This wiki is maintained by the server staff. If you have any questions or need clarification on any rules, please contact a staff member.
